How SkillHub Works

Simple steps to connect, learn, and teach in your neighborhood community

For Students

Discover and learn new skills from qualified teachers in your neighborhood

1

Search & Discover

Browse skills available in your area using our location-based search. Filter by skill level, price range, and teacher availability to find the perfect match.

2

View Teacher Profiles

Check qualifications, reviews, and availability of potential teachers. Read detailed profiles, see teaching styles and select the best fit from other students.

3

Book Sessions

Schedule convenient meeting times and locations that work for both you and your teacher. Choose from various session formats and durations.

4

Learn & Review

Attend sessions and provide feedback to help build our community of trust. Track your progress and start rating learning experiences.

For Teachers

You've created your profile! Here's how to succeed as a SkillHub teacher

1

Optimize Your Profile

Complete your profile with detailed descriptions, photos, and credentials. A well-crafted profile attracts more students and builds trust in the community.

2

Set Your Terms

Choose your rates, availability, and teaching style preferences. Define your ideal student and specify your preferred meeting locations and session formats.

3

Connect with Learners

Receive booking requests from nearby students interested in your skills. Review student profiles and confirm sessions that align with your teaching goals.

4

Teach & Grow

Build your reputation through excellent teaching and advance through teacher levels. Unlock new features, higher visibility, and premium opportunities.
