How SkillHub Works

Simple steps to connect, learn, and teach in your neighborhood community

For Sponsors

From registration to successful partnerships - our platform makes sponsorship connections simple and effective

1

Create Profile

Set up your sponsorship profile with company information. Sponsors add company information, budget range, and target audience, Event organizers share event details, expected audience demographics, and venue details.

2

Set Preferences

Define your specific sponsorship preferences. Sponsors set sponsorship categories, budget limits, and geographic preferences. Event organizers specify event themes, package tiers, and ideal sponsor types.

3

Browse & Discover

Sponsors explore sponsorship opportunities filtered by category, location, and audience. Event organizers use browse potential sponsors and view their sponsorship interests and budget ranges.

4

Connect & Partner

Initiate contact through our secure messaging system. Discuss sponsorship packages, negotiate terms, and finalize sponsorship with built-in contract and agreement tools to streamline partnerships.
