Best places to practice guitar in Kandy?

S
Sarah Johnson
Looking for quiet spaces to practice guitar? I can practice guitar without disturbing others. Any recommendations? Would be great to have a community space for musicians to practice guitar whether outside or acoustic without worrying about the noise.
M
Mike Davis
There's a great music room at the Community Center that you can book for free! Also the park near 5th street has good acoustics and is usually quiet in the mornings.
L
Lisa Torres
I've been practicing guitar in Kandy Lake area early in the morning - it's peaceful and the acoustics are amazing! The lakeside pavilion is perfect for acoustic sessions.
R
Raj Patel
Check out the Royal Botanical Gardens - they have designated areas for musicians and artists. You might need a small permit but it's usually free for locals practicing.
